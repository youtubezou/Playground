### 3.3.3

> In a string of length $$n$$, how many of the following are there?

> a) Prefixes.

$$n + 1$$

> b) Suffixes.

$$n + 1$$

> c) Proper prefixes.

$$n - 1$$

> d) Substrings.

$$\displaystyle \frac{n(n+1)}{2}+1$$

> e) Subsequences.

$$2^n$$
